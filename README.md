## 🏢 The Vision
Laura’s Legacy Suites is a branded hospitality asset in Buffalo, NY, designed to outclass 95% of local inventory through universal design, smart-tech integration, and tiered heritage branding.

## 🤝 The Mission
We are a community-first venture. **40% of all net profits** are allocated to a dedicated **Growth Fund** for the acquisition and rehabilitation of high-quality affordable housing for local Buffalo families.

## 🛏️ The Heritage Suites
- **The Sabres Suite (Anchor):** Premier sports-heritage theme for the ultimate Buffalo fan.
- **The Blue & White Room:** A sophisticated academic tribute to the University of Buffalo.
- **The Electric City Suite:** Industrial-chic honoring Buffalo's history of innovation.

## 📊 Strategic Moat
- **Universal Design:** Fully accessible stay for multi-generational travel.
- **Tiered Pricing:** Dynamic RevPAR maximization through anchor-suite premiums.
- **Safety Shield:** 9-month combined operational and debt reserves pre-funded at launch.
